About Us

At My Finance Guru, we believe that financial success is not just about earning money, but about making your money work for you. Our mission is to empower individuals and small businesses across India with expert financial and investment planning services. We understand that every financial journey is unique, which is why we provide personalized solutions tailored to your specific goals and circumstances. With our comprehensive approach to wealth creation, we help you navigate the complex world of investments, taxation, and financial planning with confidence and clarity.

Services Offered

Mutual Fund Planning

Expert guidance on selecting the right mutual funds based on your risk profile and investment goals. We help you build a diversified portfolio for long-term wealth creation.

Tax-saving Investments

Maximize your tax savings with our strategic investment planning in ELSS, PPF, NSC, and other tax-efficient instruments under Section 80C and other provisions.

Retirement Planning

Secure your golden years with our comprehensive retirement planning services. We help you build a corpus that ensures financial independence post-retirement.

Insurance Advisory

Protect your family and assets with the right insurance coverage. We provide guidance on life, health, and general insurance policies tailored to your needs.

SIP Planning

Start your investment journey with Systematic Investment Plans. We help you choose the right SIPs and amounts to achieve your financial goals systematically.

Goal-Based Financial Planning

Whether it's buying a home, funding your child's education, or planning a dream vacation, we create customized financial plans for all your life goals.

Investment Advisory

Get expert investment advice tailored to your financial goals and risk profile. We help you make informed investment decisions across various asset classes.

Stock Market Classes

Learn the fundamentals of stock market investing with our comprehensive educational classes. From basics to advanced strategies, we'll help you become a confident investor.
